Projects Completed

project 2- RC Plane

RC Plane is a plane building and flying competition conducted by Aeromodelling club of IIT Bombay , in this project we build a fully functional one motor , high wing RC Plane with all its basic components working , like aileron, rudder and elevator

Project 3- Universal Testing Machine

UTM(Universal Testing Machine)is a machine which helps us to find strength of a material by computing its stress vs strain graph using data of current change in motor, it was a group project with group of 6 students under our institute's makerspace course ,in this project i deal with design of UTM , mechanical data analysis (gear ratio , dimensions,stress, motor power), soldering and lathe manufacturing.
